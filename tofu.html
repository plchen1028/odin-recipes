MaPo Tofu Recipe

Home

Description

Here's how you can make a tasty mapo tofu dinner.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Chilli peppers
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • peppercorns
  • ground pork
  • spicy bean sauce
  • chicken stock
  • tofu
  • water
  • corn starch
  • sesame oil
  • sugar
  • scallion

Steps

  1. Heat chillis, spices, and add oil
  2. Cook in ground pork and peppercorns
  3. Add spicy bean sauce
  4. Add tofu
