ZhaJiangMian Recipe

Home

Here is how my family cooks the BeiJing style ZhaJianMian noodles

Ingredients

  • 500g all purpose flour
  • 180 ml water
  • Include a bowl of water and 3 scoops yellow bean past
  • 1 scoop black bean paste
  • 2 scoops of sweet paste.

Steps

  1. Cook pork tiny cubes.
  2. Add sesame oil and vegetable oil. Sesame and vegetable respectively is 1:3. Add pork into oils. Cook on wok.
  3. Add cooking wine.
  4. Mix huang jiang and gan jiang. 3:1. in bowl. add one spoon huang jiang fen and cooking wine 1:5.
  5. In wok with pork cubes add the huang jiang and gan jian mixture. Cook 25 minutes. Add jiang fen and cooking wine periodically. Add around a spoon of sugar and some sesame oil.
