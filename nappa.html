Nappa Cabbage Recipe

Home

Description

Here's how you can make a chinese nappa dish the traditional way.

Ingredients

  • 1 (2-3 lb / 1-1.2 kg) small head napa cabbage
  • 1 and 1/2 tablespoons peanut oil
  • 4 chili peppers , dried
  • 1 inch ginger , minced
  • 1 clove garlic , minced
  • 2 tablespoons chinkiang vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

Steps

  1. Separate the leaves from the head of cabbage. Cut off the leafy green parts and save them in a ziplock bag for future use (*Footnote 1). Tilt your knife to slice the cabbage at a 45° angle into 1” (2.5 cm) pieces. (See blog post above for detailed pictures)
  2. Combine the sauce ingredients in a small bowl and stir to mix well.
  3. In a large carbon steel skillet or a wok, heat the oil over medium-high heat until hot. Add the dried chili peppers. Once the chilis start to brown, add the ginger, garlic, and green onions. Saute until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Add the sliced cabbage and turn to high heat. Cook without moving for 30 seconds. Then saute for 2 minutes, or until the cabbage begins to soften but is still crunchy. Do not overcook the cabbage, which will cause it to release liquid and make the sauce watery.
  5. Immediately transfer everything to a big serving plate and serve it hot as a side dish.
